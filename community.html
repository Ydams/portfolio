The Builders Circle
A space for builders, entrepreneurs, and doers who move with intention
Live

The Builders Circle is a curated WhatsApp community for entrepreneurs, product builders, and growth-minded professionals who are serious about execution. This isn't just another group — it's a focused space where ideas meet action.

Every month, members get access to live webinar sessions hosted by Damilola Abraham covering growth strategies, product thinking, digital operations, and career development. Conversations are real, resources are practical, and the network is intentional.

If you're building something — a business, a career, a product, or yourself — this is where you belong.

What You Get
  • Monthly live webinar sessions with Damilola Abraham on growth, mindset & execution
  • Exclusive access to resources, templates, and frameworks shared only in the group
  • Peer-to-peer accountability with fellow builders who are serious about results
  • Early announcements for events, opportunities, and collaborations
  • Real-time conversations, feedback, and support from an active community
  • Free to join — always open to ambitious individuals ready to take action

DAF Volunteers Connect
Powered by the Damilola Abraham Foundation — for those who give back
Live
Community

DAF Volunteers Connect is the official community group of the Damilola Abraham Foundation — a purpose-driven space for volunteers, change-makers, and individuals committed to creating real impact in their communities.

Whether you're a seasoned volunteer or just beginning your journey of service, this group connects you with like-minded people who believe in empowerment through digital skills, mentorship, and community outreach.

As a member, you'll be among the first to know about upcoming DAF initiatives, training programs, outreach events, and volunteer opportunities across Nigeria and beyond.

What You Get
  • First access to DAF training programs, digital skills bootcamps, and outreach events
  • Volunteer opportunities to directly impact individuals and communities
  • Mentorship coordination and access to growth support systems
  • Connect with fellow volunteers who are passionate about community development
  • Updates on DAF initiatives, partnerships, and impact projects as they happen
  • A supportive space to share ideas, collaborate, and build your network of impact
Free to join · Open to all · DAF Foundation initiative

Join in 3 Steps

Getting into the community is simple. Click, join, and start connecting right away.

01
Choose Your Group
Pick the community that matches where you are and where you're going — The Builders Circle for growth-focused professionals, or DAF Volunteers Connect for community impact.
02
Click the Join Button
Tap the green WhatsApp button on the community card. You'll be taken directly to the WhatsApp invite link. No sign-up forms, no waiting — instant access.
03
Introduce Yourself
Once you're in, say hello! Share your name, what you do, and what you're building or working towards. The community is warm, active, and always ready to engage.
