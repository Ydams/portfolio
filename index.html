01 — Healthcare
MediFlow Patient Portal
UX Research Figma Prototyping React Native Usability Testing
Duration
4 months

Problem Statement

Patients at a major hospital network struggled with a fragmented digital experience. The existing app had a 71% abandonment rate on the appointment booking flow, with users citing confusion, excessive steps, and trust issues around medical data.

My Role

Lead UI/UX Designer working alongside a product manager and a 3-person engineering team. I owned research, information architecture, interaction design, and design system implementation.

Design Process

Conducted 14 user interviews and a competitive audit of 8 healthcare apps. Synthesized findings into 3 user personas and journey maps. Ran 2 rounds of usability testing on lo-fi prototypes before high-fidelity implementation.

Key Decisions

Reduced the booking flow from 11 steps to 4. Introduced a persistent progress indicator, clearer data consent UI, and a "care timeline" view that unified all health records in a single scroll.

02 — FinTech
Navi Finance Dashboard
Product Design Data Viz Design System Next.js Tailwind
Duration
6 months

Problem Statement

Young professionals in Ghana lacked accessible tools for tracking personal finances. Existing solutions were either too complex, didn't support local currencies, or failed to account for informal income patterns common in the market.

My Role

End-to-end product designer and front-end engineer. Built the full design system in Figma, created interactive prototypes, and implemented the React/Next.js front-end with real-time data visualization components.

Design Process

Surveyed 80+ users aged 22-35. Identified key mental models around money management in the Ghanaian context. Designed a contextual onboarding that auto-detects income patterns. Built a 64-component design system for consistency and developer handoff.

Technical Implementation

Built with Next.js 14, Recharts for data visualization, and Zustand for state management. Achieved 97 Lighthouse performance score. Designed accessible colour contrast at WCAG AA across all components.
